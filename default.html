For all the sudoku lovers out there, now theres an app for when you get stuck. Sudoku Breaker is able to solve any puzzle out there, from a tiny 4x4, to a gigantic 12x12 puzzle. Puzzle Breaker

Breaking sudoku puzzles.

Puzzle Breaker is an iOS Application for solving sudoku puzzles. Simply enter the puzzle on the board and watch it solve for you! It has options to solve the entire puzzle, give hints, or solve just the penicil marks.
